Mckleene Premium Products, Inc. was first registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) on May 10, 1989 as a manufacturer and trader of janitorial supplies and equipment.

In January 02, 1990, Mckleene expanded its operation by providing janitorial services, building maintenance and allied services. On May 1997, Mckleene added another services as follows: septic tank excavation (pozonegro) and termites treatment (pest control).

Furthermore, Mckleene has secured its permits and licenses to operate as follows: (1) The Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) on June 16, 1989 for the Certificate of Registration for Business Name which is renewable every five (5) years; (2) The City of San Fernando (La Union) on January 02, 1990 for the City Permits & Licenses which is renewable annually; (3) The Department of Health (DOH) for the Health Permit; and has secured a Countryside Barangay Business Enterprise (CBEE) Certificate.

Mckleene has been in business as a manufacturer, trader and service agency for more than twenty (20) years. At present, 		  Mckleene is the biggest and the leading janitorial and manpower service agency in Northern Luzon. Mckleene has established its branch in Baguio City and satellite office in Dagupan City, servicing the private and government agencies in Northern Luzon.

With more than twenty (20) years in operations, Mckleene has already established its name in Regions I, II, III, CAR and now at NCR.
